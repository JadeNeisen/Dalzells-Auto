Welcome

Since 1958, Dalzell's Auto Repair has stayed current with equipment and has committed to ongoing training which allows us to provide our customers with exceptional service. The one reason for our success is that our customers trust us to do a very good job at a fair price. Today, we are once again striving to stay on the forefront of the new technology and the demands of vehicles both new and old with our new location at 8935-127 ave in Edmonton, Alberta. This new facility is fully equipped with state-of-the-art computer technology for today's automobiles.

The traditions continue among all the staff, and we're very proud to say that we only employ licensed or apprentice mechanics. Our clients can rest assured that the staff working on their cars are fully trained and know what they are doing.
